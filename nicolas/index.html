About

My journey into programming began in 2010, at just 14 years old, when I became fascinated with touchscreen devices and started exploring how the apps I used on my iPod touch were made. That curiosity quickly grew into a deeper commitment, leading me to create Techno-Magic as a storefront for my development projects.

Beyond coding, I’m a world traveler and a polyglot—I’m a native bilingual in English and Spanish , with proficiency in Italian after studying abroad in Florence. I make a point of traveling at least a couple of times a year to explore new cultures and experiences.

To balance my immersion in the tech world, I enjoy diving into a good book, listening to progressive house music, and studying design and typography. I also love engaging in deep, meaningful conversations with my wife, and spending time in nature together while drinking mate. In the evenings, there’s nothing better than unwinding with a captivating TV show by her side.
