About Nico

I am a self-taught app developer, web developer, and graphic designer. As you read this, I’m either programming something or thinking about it. Sometimes I write poems.

I’m majoring in Software Engineering and minoring in Digital Arts at UC Irvine. I fluently speak, read, and write in both English, and Spanish. Parlo un po’ di Italiano.

I’ve worked on websites for as long as I can remember, and started developing for iOS when I got my first Mac, back in mid-2009. I founded Techno-Magic in early-2010 to serve as a storefront for my development adventures. Check me out on LinkedIn for other things I’ve worked on.

I can be found frequently on Twitter and occasionally on App.net. Sometimes you’ll find me posting photos on Instagram. I also push some of my code, and contribute to a few projects on GitHub from time to time.

If you need to contact me, email nicolas@gomollon.me or tweet @gomollon.
